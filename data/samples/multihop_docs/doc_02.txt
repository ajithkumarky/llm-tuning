The NovaPad tablet was developed by TechVista Inc in 2022 as the company's flagship consumer product. Designed from the ground up to serve as both a productivity tool and an entertainment device, the NovaPad features a 12.4-inch OLED display with a resolution of 2800 by 1752 pixels, delivering vivid colors and deep blacks that rival devices costing twice as much. The tablet runs on a custom-optimized version of Android with TechVista's proprietary interface layer called FlowUI, which simplifies multitasking and introduces gesture-based navigation tailored for the larger screen format.

One of the NovaPad's most notable features is its battery life. Using TechVista's patented PowerSustain technology, the tablet achieves up to sixteen hours of continuous use on a single charge, a figure that was independently verified by several technology review outlets. The device also supports rapid charging, reaching eighty percent capacity in just thirty-five minutes with the included 45-watt charger. The NovaPad weighs 480 grams and measures just 5.9 millimeters in thickness, making it one of the lightest tablets in its size class. It comes in three storage configurations: 128, 256, and 512 gigabytes, with prices starting at 349 dollars. Since its launch, the NovaPad has sold over two million units worldwide and received an average rating of 4.6 out of 5 stars across major retail platforms. TechVista has announced that a second-generation NovaPad with 5G connectivity is currently in development and expected to ship in late 2025.
