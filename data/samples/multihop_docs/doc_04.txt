BrightPath Solutions specializes in renewable energy consulting and the development of advanced energy storage systems for commercial and residential applications. The company was established in 2018 by a group of three engineers who previously worked together at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory in Golden, Colorado. Headquartered in Portland, Oregon, BrightPath has grown from its original founding team to a workforce of over one hundred and twenty employees spread across offices in Portland, San Diego, and Boston. The company's core mission is to accelerate the transition to sustainable energy by providing practical, cost-effective storage solutions that address the intermittency challenges of solar and wind power.

BrightPath's consulting division works with municipal governments and large commercial property owners to design and implement microgrid systems that combine on-site renewable generation with battery storage. To date, the company has completed over two hundred consulting engagements across fifteen states, helping clients reduce their energy costs by an average of thirty-five percent. The engineering division focuses on proprietary battery technology, and BrightPath holds twenty-seven patents related to solid-state electrolyte chemistry and thermal regulation of battery packs. In 2023, BrightPath closed a Series B funding round of forty-five million dollars led by GreenHorizon Capital, valuing the company at approximately three hundred million dollars. The company was named to Fast Company's list of most innovative energy companies in both 2022 and 2023, and it has established research partnerships with Oregon State University and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
