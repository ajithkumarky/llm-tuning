DataForge Analytics provides AI-powered business intelligence solutions to mid-sized and enterprise-level companies across a range of industries including finance, healthcare, retail, and logistics. The company was founded in 2020 by twin brothers Alex and Michael Torres in Chicago, Illinois. Both founders had previously worked as data scientists at major consulting firms and grew frustrated with the gap between the promise of artificial intelligence and the reality of how most businesses were actually using their data. They started DataForge with the goal of building tools that would allow companies without large in-house data science teams to harness the power of machine learning for strategic decision-making.

DataForge operates on a software-as-a-service model, offering its platform through tiered subscription plans that scale based on data volume and the number of users. The company's annual recurring revenue surpassed twenty million dollars in 2024, and it currently serves over three hundred corporate clients. DataForge employs approximately ninety people, with its engineering team split between the Chicago headquarters and a satellite office in Toronto, Canada. The company has raised a total of thirty-two million dollars in venture capital across two funding rounds, with the most recent Series A of twenty million dollars closing in early 2023. DataForge was recognized by Gartner as a Cool Vendor in analytics and business intelligence in 2023, and the Torres brothers were named to Forbes' 30 Under 30 list in the enterprise technology category. The company's guiding principle is that data-driven insights should be accessible to every decision-maker, not just those with technical expertise.
