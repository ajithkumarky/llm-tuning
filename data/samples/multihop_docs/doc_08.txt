The InsightEngine platform was built by DataForge Analytics as an all-in-one business intelligence solution designed to transform raw corporate data into actionable strategic insights. First released in 2021, InsightEngine connects to a wide variety of data sources including SQL databases, cloud data warehouses like Snowflake and BigQuery, spreadsheet files, and popular SaaS platforms such as Salesforce, HubSpot, and Shopify. Once connected, the platform uses proprietary natural language processing technology to allow users to ask questions about their data in plain English, receiving answers in the form of interactive charts, summary reports, and predictive forecasts.

The platform's core differentiator is its AutoModel feature, which automatically identifies patterns, trends, and anomalies in a company's data without requiring users to have any knowledge of statistics or machine learning. AutoModel runs continuous background analysis and surfaces relevant findings through a notification system that alerts users when significant changes or opportunities are detected. InsightEngine also includes a collaborative workspace where team members can share dashboards, annotate findings, and build presentation-ready reports. The platform currently supports integration with over seventy data sources and processes an average of two billion data points per day across its client base. Pricing starts at 500 dollars per month for the Starter tier, which supports up to ten users and five data source connections. Enterprise clients on custom plans can access advanced features including dedicated compute resources, custom model training, and priority support. InsightEngine has received consistently high ratings on G2 and Capterra, with users particularly praising its ease of setup and the quality of its automated insights.
