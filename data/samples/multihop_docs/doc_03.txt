Dr. James Park is the lead engineer at TechVista Inc, where he oversees all hardware development and heads the company's advanced research division. He joined TechVista in early 2020, just months after the company was founded, making him one of the first ten employees. Dr. Park holds a Ph.D. in electrical engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology, where his doctoral research focused on low-power display technologies and energy-efficient processor architectures. Before joining TechVista, he spent six years at Samsung's display division in South Korea, where he contributed to the development of several generations of AMOLED panels used in flagship smartphones.

At TechVista, Dr. Park led the engineering team that developed the NovaPad's PowerSustain battery management system, which has become one of the company's most valuable intellectual properties. He holds nineteen patents personally and is listed as a co-inventor on an additional eight filed by TechVista. Dr. Park is known within the company for his hands-on leadership style. He regularly works alongside junior engineers in the prototyping lab, and he conducts a monthly technical workshop series that has become a staple of TechVista's internal training program. Outside of work, Dr. Park is an active member of the IEEE and has published over thirty peer-reviewed papers on topics ranging from flexible display substrates to thermal management in compact electronic devices. He lives in Austin with his wife and two children and is an avid rock climber who frequently visits the climbing areas around Enchanted Rock State Natural Area.
