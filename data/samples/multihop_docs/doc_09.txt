Raj Patel is a senior data scientist at DataForge Analytics, where he plays a central role in developing the machine learning models that power the InsightEngine platform's AutoModel feature. He joined the company in 2021 as one of its first dedicated research hires and has since become one of the most influential technical contributors on the team. Raj holds a Master of Science in computer science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where he specialized in natural language processing and statistical learning. Before joining DataForge, he worked for three years as a machine learning engineer at a healthcare analytics startup in Boston, where he built predictive models for patient readmission risk and treatment outcome forecasting.

At DataForge, Raj led the development of the natural language query engine that allows InsightEngine users to interact with their data using conversational English rather than SQL or programming languages. This feature has been widely cited as one of the primary reasons clients choose InsightEngine over competing products. Raj has also spearheaded the company's efforts in time-series anomaly detection, building algorithms that can identify unusual patterns in financial and operational data with high precision and low false-positive rates. He has published several papers at top machine learning conferences including NeurIPS and ICML, and he maintains a popular technical blog where he writes about practical applications of AI in business settings. Raj lives in Chicago's Lincoln Park neighborhood, where he enjoys running along the lakefront trail and volunteering as a coding instructor at a local after-school program for high school students.
